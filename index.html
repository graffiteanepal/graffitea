Who We Are

A Cozy Corner for Honest Drinks

Graffitea is a small local Kathmandu shop built around familiar comfort: fresh tea on the boil, welcoming service, and drinks made with care rather than shortcuts.

Every tea is brewed fresh daily, and every cold press juice is made in small batches with zero additives, so what reaches your cup or bottle stays simple, clean, and true to the ingredients.
